Vacancies

At Karlstad University, you have more than a thousand colleagues. Colleagues who support and strengthen each other are always close by, as are other departments, subjects and research groups. We develop together.

The university looks forward, as is evident in everything we do. There is always room for your ideas and enthusiasm.

Each morning you see throngs of people: students, staff and visitors from all parts of the world. Hearing five different languages spoken in a single day is not unusual. You will meet our students in the corridors, at the library, in the cafeteria and at the concerts they give.
We all contribute and so will you.
